## First Time Setup

### 1. Open the App
- Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox)
- Navigate to the app URL
- You'll see the main scanning interface

### 2. Camera Permission
When you first use the camera scanner:
- Your browser will ask for camera permission
- Click **"Allow"** to enable barcode scanning
- If you accidentally denied, you can change this in your browser settings

### 3. Sign In (Optional)
For the best experience, create an account:
- Click the **Account** button (person icon) in the top-right
- Choose one of three options:
  - **Continue Without Account**: Start immediately (anonymous mode)
  - **Sign In**: Use an existing email/password account
  - **Sign Up**: Create a new account with email and password

**Benefits of signing in:**
- Your scans sync across all your devices
- Your history is saved even if you clear browser data
- Works offline and syncs when you're back online

## Scanning Barcodes

### Camera Scanning

1. **Select the Camera Tab**
   - The "Use webcam" tab should be selected by default
   - If not, click the camera icon tab

2. **Position the Barcode**
   - Hold your product with the barcode visible
   - Position the barcode within the white scan frame
   - Keep the barcode steady and well-lit

3. **Automatic Detection**
   - The scanner works automatically - no button to press!
   - You'll see a scan frame overlay on the video
   - When a barcode is detected, you'll get visual and audio feedback

4. **Advanced Camera Controls**
   - **Torch/Flash**: Click the lightning bolt icon for better lighting
   - **Camera Selection**: If you have multiple cameras, select from the dropdown
   - **Zoom**: Use + and - buttons to zoom in/out

### Image Scanning

1. **Select the Image Tab**
   - Click the "Use image" tab

2. **Upload an Image**
   - **Drag & Drop**: Drag an image file onto the dropzone
   - **Click to Select**: Click the dropzone to choose a file from your device
   - Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF

3. **View Results**
   - The image will appear with a preview
   - The barcode will be automatically detected and displayed

## Understanding Scan Results

### Scan Confirmation Dialog

After scanning a barcode (if enabled in settings), you'll see a confirmation dialog:

#### Product Information
- **Product Image**: Visual preview of the item
- **Product Name**: The name of the scanned product
- **Brand**: The manufacturer or brand name
- **Description**: Additional product details
- **Barcode**: The actual barcode number

#### Setting Expiration Date
- **Expiration Date Field**: Click to set a specific date
- **Default**: If you don't set a date, it defaults to 30 days from scan
- **Custom**: Choose any future date based on your needs

#### Adding Notes
- **Notes Field**: Add custom notes about this item
- Examples: "Opened on [date]", "Keep refrigerated", "Left side of pantry"

#### Save or Cancel
- **Save to History**: Add this item to your scan history
- **Cancel**: Discard this scan without saving

## Using Scan History

### Accessing History
- Click the **History** button (clock icon) in the top-right
- A sidebar will open showing all your scanned items

### Search Functionality
1. **Search Box**: Type to search by:
   - Product name
   - Brand name
   - Barcode number
   - Description

2. **Real-time Results**: Results update as you type

### Filter by Status
Use the filter chips to quickly view:
- **All**: Show everything
- **Fresh**: Items with more than 7 days until expiration (green)
- **Expiring Soon**: Items expiring within 7 days (yellow/orange)
- **Expired**: Items past their expiration date (red)

### Viewing Item Details
1. **Click any item** in the history list
2. A detailed view opens showing:
   - Large product image
   - Complete product information
   - Expiration countdown with visual status
   - Your custom notes
   - Scan date and barcode format

### Managing History
- **Copy Barcode**: Click the copy icon next to any item
- **Delete Item**: Click the trash icon to remove a single item
- **Empty History**: Scroll to bottom and click "Empty history" to clear all

## Tips & Tricks

### Getting Better Scans

1. **Lighting**
   - Use the torch/flash in dark environments
   - Avoid glare and reflections on shiny barcodes
   - Natural daylight works best

2. **Distance**
   - Start farther away and move closer
   - Use zoom if the barcode is small
   - Keep the barcode parallel to the camera

3. **Stability**
   - Hold your phone/device steady
   - Rest your hand on a surface if possible
   - Let the camera focus before scanning

### Organizing Your History

1. **Use Notes Effectively**
   - Add location: "Top shelf", "Freezer door"
   - Add opening date: "Opened 12/1"
   - Add reminders: "Use in smoothies"

2. **Set Accurate Expiration Dates**
   - Check actual package dates
   - Adjust for opened items (shorter life)
   - Be conservative to avoid waste

3. **Regular Reviews**
   - Filter by "Expiring Soon" daily
   - Plan meals around expiring items
   - Delete used items to keep history clean

### Offline Usage

1. **Keep Scanning Offline**
   - Scans are saved locally immediately
   - Product details may be limited
   - Everything syncs when online again

2. **Sync Management**
   - Green toast: Successfully synced X items
   - Orange toast: Offline mode active
   - Automatic sync when connection returns

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Issues

**Problem**: Camera not working
- **Check permissions**: Allow camera access in browser settings
- **Try different camera**: Use the camera selector dropdown
- **Reload page**: Refresh the browser
- **Try another browser**: Some browsers work better than others

**Problem**: Barcode not detecting
- **Better lighting**: Use torch or move to brighter area
- **Clean lens**: Wipe camera lens
- **Adjust distance**: Move closer or farther
- **Try zoom**: Use zoom controls for small barcodes

### Scanning Issues

**Problem**: Wrong product detected
- **Try again**: Scan the barcode a second time
- **Check barcode**: Ensure barcode is not damaged
- **Manual entry**: Cancel and enter barcode manually (if supported)

**Problem**: No product information
- **Not all barcodes in database**: Some products aren't in the database
- **Add manually**: Use the notes field to add information
- **Generic UPC**: Store brands may not have details

### Account & Sync Issues

**Problem**: Not syncing across devices
- **Check sign-in**: Ensure you're signed in with same account
- **Check internet**: Verify connection on both devices
- **Wait for sync**: Give it a few minutes
- **Refresh**: Close and reopen the app

**Problem**: Lost history
- **Check account**: Sign in to recover cloud data
- **Check device**: History is device-specific if not signed in
- **Recent delete**: Check if you accidentally emptied history

### Performance Issues

**Problem**: App running slowly
- **Clear old scans**: Delete expired or unnecessary items
- **Close other tabs**: Free up browser resources
- **Update browser**: Use latest browser version
- **Restart device**: Clear memory
